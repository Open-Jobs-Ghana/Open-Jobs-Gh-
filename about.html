Who are we and what is our purpose

Open is a tool created to help you look for the right job easily at the comfort of your own home. Our main goal is to ensure that you find the right job, talent and internship programs tailored to suite your needs.

At open, we believe that everyone deserves a chance to hire and be hired. If you are open for work, we’ve got you. And if you have a position open that needs the right talent, we’ve got you too.
