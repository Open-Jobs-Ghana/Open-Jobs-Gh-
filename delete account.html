Delete Account

This action is permanent

Deleting your account removes your profile from Open immediately and permanently. There's no recovery period and no way to undo this once it's confirmed.

  • Your profile, name, username, and login details
  • Every job, gig, or listing you've posted
  • Your saved biometric sign-in on this device

If you've messaged other users, those conversations stay visible to them, but your name and contact details will no longer be attached to your account.
